About Me

I am a Computer Engineering Student at the University of Waterloo. In complete honesty, I don't really know anything about anything. The more I learn as a student, the more intrigued I am by how little we actually know. I am deeply fascinated by philosophical questions regarding our nature of existence. My constant seeking has brought me to practice quieting my thoughts, opening my heart and listening to the intuitive mind. In my free time, you can find me writing gibberish in my journal or having long discussions with my friends. I also work on some software projects for practice and fun.
